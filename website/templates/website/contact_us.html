This Website

The idea of this website is to provide people with all the necessary information together with access to tests to allow them to treat their own illnesses by identifying the underlying processes that are resulting in their ill health. The majority of people that follow this route recover without consulting me directly.

In the past I have done my best to offer new patient consultations to people who have got stuck along the way because their problem does not resolve simply. However I am currently overloaded with my current patients, so for the time being please go to the list of practitioners of Ecological Medicine by visiting the website of The British Society for Ecological Medicine (BSEM). A list of such practitioners (who specialise in allergy, environmental and nutritional medicine) is also published on this website - Practitioners of Ecological (Allergy, Environmental, Nutritional) Medicine. Please see also my new Practitioner Portal - Natural Health Worldwide

This combined approach is the most time efficient and cost efficient way of helping as many people as I can! Much of the basic approach (see The general approach to maintaining and restoring good health ) does not require medical input and many people recover their health simply by putting these recommendations in place.

If you find your problem is not addressed by this website, please let me know so I can put in the relevant information.

Acute Medical Problems

Please, do not send me inquiries about acute medical problems, i.e. sudden serious symptoms. I am not in a position to reply to each query personally and give medical advice. I am the only doctor in my practice and cannot be available 24/7. If you are experiencing unusual or new symptoms which worry you, you must report them to your GP/medical practitioner and/or to your local hospital Accident and Emergency Department.
